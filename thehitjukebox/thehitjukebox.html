back

what you see here is the interface of the hitjukebox.
this image shows the positive feedback a user receives after entering a very popular song. the song playing will immediately be switched to the one just evaluated by the app.
this image shows the negative feedback a user receives after entering a song that is less popular than the one currently beeing looped by the app. this song will not be played by the hitjukebox.
this image shows the feedback a user gets when resetting the song currently playing to the song that is streamed the most via spotify on the current day.

this jukebox app only plays the most popular songs in order to let most of the people have a good time.
